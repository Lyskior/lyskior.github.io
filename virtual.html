Virtual Tour & Admissions Chatbot

This project combines an interactive virtual school tour with a chatbot designed to help prospective students and families navigate information more easily. I built it in response to a real problem I noticed: students and parents new to the school often struggle to understand the campus layout and find answers to practical questions. The goal was to create a more accessible and engaging way to explore the school while making information available in a format that feels intuitive and useful.

  • Built the overall system and interface
  • Focused on usability and clear user experience
  • Connected the project to a real admissions-related need
