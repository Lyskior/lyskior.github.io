VEX Robotics (3 Years)

Over three years in VEX Robotics, I progressed from learning the basics of robot construction to taking on more advanced roles in design, programming, and leadership. Through the build season, competitions, and iterative testing, I developed practical engineering skills while learning how to solve technical problems as part of a team.

Experience Breakdown

Year 1

Focused on fundamentals such as robot assembly, understanding game strategy, and learning how mechanisms contribute to performance.

Year 2

Took on more responsibility in design and iteration, improving mechanisms and contributing more actively to engineering decisions.

Year 3

Worked at a higher technical level with greater emphasis on advanced systems, optimization, and leadership during the season.
