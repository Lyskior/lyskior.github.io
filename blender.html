Blender Club Leader

Blender Club is a student-led space for 3D design, modeling, and animation. As leader, I organize meetings, teach technical skills, and help members move from learning the basics to creating their own projects. The club combines creativity with practical digital tools, giving members space to experiment while building real design ability.
